Pair your coffee with our delectable pastries, sandwiches, and desserts, all made in-house with the finest ingredients. The History of Nice Coffee

Nice Coffee began with a simple dream: to bring joy through a perfect cup of coffee. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, had a profound passion for the world of coffee and the unique flavors of the finest coffee beans from around the globe. It was this passion that fueled her to embark on this remarkable journey.

In 2010, Sarah decided to open her small café on a quiet street corner in the heart of the city. She started with a small but loyal customer base who came in every day to savor her dedicated craftsmanship. Sarah always strived for the highest quality in everything, from coffee beans to customer service.

Over time, Nice Coffee began to gain a reputation not just as a place that served the best coffee but also as a friendly and passionate community. The café became a gathering spot to share stories, ideas, and smiles at every corner.

Nice Coffee continued to grow and brought innovation to the world of coffee. We sourced coffee beans from local and international farmers committed to sustainable practices. We also trained our baristas to create art in every cup we served.

Today, Nice Coffee is a prime destination for coffee enthusiasts looking to experience exceptional flavors. Despite our growth, our commitment to delivering a special coffee experience remains the same. We feel fortunate to share our passion with you, our loyal customers, and with everyone seeking a nice place to enjoy a cup of coffee.

Thank you for your support over the years. We are committed to continuing to bring joy through coffee at Nice Coffee, where coffee meets happiness.

In 2015

Nice Coffee expanded its menu to include a wider range of exotic and innovative coffee beverages. This included drinks like Nitro Cold Brew and Espresso Tonic, which quickly became customer favorites. We also began hosting coffee events and workshops to introduce more people to the art and culture of coffee.

In 2018

Nice Coffee committed to sustainability. We partnered with sustainable coffee farmers and worked to reduce our environmental footprint. We transitioned to eco-friendly packaging and minimized waste as much as possible. Additionally, we launched the "Coffee for Good" program, where a portion of our earnings was donated to support coffee farming communities worldwide.

To this day

Nice Coffee has grown into a café network with multiple locations in various cities. While our scale has expanded, our commitment to serving high-quality coffee and providing exceptional customer experiences remains unchanged.
